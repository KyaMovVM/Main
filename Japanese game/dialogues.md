# Dialogues / ダイアログ

Диалоги визуальной новеллы.

## Scene 0 - Introduction

**Yuki**: Good morning! This is a Visual Novel demo. Choose your path.

- Choice 1: Talk to her
- Choice 2: Ignore her

## Scene 1 - Talk Response

**Yuki**: Oh, you decided to talk to me! That's great!

- Choice 1: Ask about her day
- Choice 2: Go to next class

## Scene 2 - Ignore Response

**Yuki**: Oh... I see. Maybe another time then.

- Choice 1: Go to next class
- Choice 2: Come back

## Scene 3 - Rooftop Talk

**Yuki**: My day was wonderful, especially after talking to you!

- Choice 1: Continue

## Scene 4 - Class

**Teacher**: Welcome to class everyone! Today we will learn about JavaScript.

- Choice 1: Pay attention
- Choice 2: Sleep

## Scene 5 - Good Ending

**Narrator**: Good ending! You made the right choices and the story came to a good conclusion.

- Choice 1: Start Over

## Scene 6 - Bad Ending

**Teacher**: You! Wake up! How dare you sleep in my class!

- Choice 1: Start Over
